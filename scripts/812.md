### [download](812.ps1)

Moving a VMware offline VM within the VI without changing its Datastore

```posh
$vm = get-vm testvm
$ds = $vm | get-datastore
move-vm -VM $vm -Destination (get-vmhost MyDestination) -Datastore $ds
```
